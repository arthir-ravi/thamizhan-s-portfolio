MARKETPLACE

Local Buying & Selling Platform

I developed a local buying and selling marketplace platform that connects buyers and sellers through a fast, intuitive, and user friendly experience. Users can create listings, browse products by category and city, and communicate directly with sellers through real time messaging, making local commerce simple and accessible.

The platform includes advanced product discovery with category filters, keyword search, and location based browsing to help users quickly find relevant listings. Sellers can easily publish, manage, and promote their products while buyers enjoy a seamless browsing and communication experience.

To support a wider audience, the marketplace offers English, Persian, and Pashto with full RTL compatibility, responsive design, and scalable architecture. These features provide an inclusive, reliable, and modern marketplace experience for both buyers and sellers.

DISTINCTIVE FEATURES

Category Based Listings

Organize and browse products through structured categories for faster and easier discovery.

City Aware Product Discovery

Discover nearby products using location-aware browsing and city specific listings.

Real Time Buyer & Seller Chat

Enable instant communication between buyers and sellers for faster negotiations and transactions.
