AI LEAD OUTREACH

AI-Powered B2B Lead Nurturing Platform

I developed an AI Lead Outreach Automation System, an end-to-end B2B automation solution that streamlines lead nurturing and partnership campaigns. Built using Clay, n8n, and Supabase, the platform collects, enriches, and organizes lead data before passing it to an AI Agent for intelligent analysis.

The AI Agent evaluates company information, services, reviews, and business stage to generate personalized outreach content for each prospect. Automated workflows deliver professional emails through Gmail and tailored WhatsApp messages via the Evolution API, ensuring context aware communication across multiple channels.

The solution eliminates repetitive manual outreach tasks while enabling personalized engagement at scale. By automating the complete lead outreach process, it helps businesses improve efficiency, strengthen prospect relationships, and accelerate business development.

AI LEAD OUTREACH FEATURES

Lead Collection & Enrichment

Collects and enriches lead data from multiple sources to build a high quality prospect database.

AI Powered Personalization

Analyzes company insights to generate personalized partnership emails and WhatsApp messages.

Lead Database Management

Stores and organizes enriched lead information securely in Supabase for easy access and automation.
