AI Customer Engagement & Service Automation

I built an AI-powered customer engagement and service automation solution for Stree F&B, a company specializing in coffee machines, vending solutions, premium beverages, and related equipment. The platform automates customer communication across Email and WhatsApp, intelligently handling enquiries, sales conversations, and service requests through AI driven workflows.

The solution integrates multiple communication channels into a unified platform, allowing customer requests to be processed, tracked, and managed automatically. AI-powered automation reduces manual intervention while ensuring timely, accurate, and consistent responses throughout the customer journey.

The implementation improved customer engagement, simplified service management, and optimized daily operations by reducing response times and enabling the support team to focus on higher value tasks, providing a scalable communication solution as the business continues to grow.
