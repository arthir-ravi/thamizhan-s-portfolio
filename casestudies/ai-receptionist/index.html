AI RECEPTIONIST

Voice Agent for Hospitals & Clinics

I developed an AI Receptionist Voice Agent for hospitals and clinics that automates patient interactions through intelligent voice conversations. Built using Vapi.ai and n8n, the solution enables patients to book appointments, check doctor availability, and request healthcare information without requiring manual assistance.

The platform leverages OpenAI GPT-4o for natural conversations, Azure Transcriber for accurate speech recognition, and Twilio for seamless call management. It integrates with Google Calendar to provide real-time appointment scheduling and availability checks, ensuring a smooth and reliable booking experience for patients.

To streamline post-call operations, the system automatically generates call summaries and securely transfers conversation data through n8n webhooks. Automated email notifications keep both hospital staff and patients informed, creating an efficient end-to-end workflow that improves communication, reduces administrative effort, and enhances the overall patient experience.

AI RECEPTIONIST FEATURES

Appointment Booking

Schedules patient visits through real-time Google Calendar availability.

Doctor Availability

Answers availability questions instantly during natural voice calls.

Call Summaries

Sends automated summaries and notifications to staff and patients.
