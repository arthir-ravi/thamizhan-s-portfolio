ABOUT MISOLAH

I developed Misolah, an intelligent marketplace that connects event organizers with expert photographers, making the hiring process simple, transparent, and dependable. Organizers can publish event requirements, receive competitive bids, and choose based on verified profiles, curated portfolios, and authentic client reviews, ensuring trusted relationships and smooth collaboration.

For photographers, Misolah doubles as a business and branding tool with SEO-optimized listings, portfolio showcases, and profile management to boost visibility. I also integrated real time messaging, instant notifications, and promotional features to simplify communication and booking.

By combining event posting, bidding, and marketing capabilities into a single scalable platform, Misolah helps creatives grow their reach, increase revenue, and focus on delivering excellent work while giving clients a stress free hiring experience for weddings, corporate events, and private parties.
