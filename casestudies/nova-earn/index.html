ABOUT NOVAEARN

I developed a customer engagement platform that transforms loyalty points into QR based vouchers, enabling businesses to boost retention, drive repeat purchases, and enhance in store interactions through a seamless rewards ecosystem.

developed for multi location operations, the platform supports real time points tracking, instant voucher conversion, and secure QR redemption at checkout. I designed its workflow to be fraud resistant, with real time data syncing and a scalable architecture to ensure smooth, reliable performance.

To help brands go beyond transactions, I integrated promotional tools such as flyers, banners, and in app offers, making it easy to run targeted campaigns, attract customers, and increase footfall. By combining a frictionless user experience with powerful business insights, the platform empowers companies to strengthen customer loyalty, maximize revenue, and achieve long term growth in competitive markets.
