ODOO AI BUSINESS REPORTER

WhatsApp Business Intelligence & Sales Reporting

I developed an Odoo AI Business Reporter, an AI powered automation solution that enables business owners and store managers to access real time business insights directly through WhatsApp. The platform integrates WhatsApp, an AI Agent, MCP Client, MCP Server, and Odoo ERP to retrieve business information using natural language queries.

The AI Agent processes requests such as top selling products, daily sales, weekly revenue, stock availability, purchase details, and store performance, then retrieves the required data from Odoo and delivers concise reports through WhatsApp conversations.

Designed for multi store businesses, the solution provides instant access to sales and operational insights without requiring users to log in to the Odoo dashboard, enabling faster decision making and improved business efficiency.

DISTINCTIVE FEATURES

Real-Time Odoo Sync

Access live sales, inventory, and business data directly from the Odoo ERP system.

Conversational Business Intelligence

Ask business questions in natural language and receive AI generated insights instantly through WhatsApp.

Smart Sales & Revenue Reports

Generate summarized reports for sales, revenue, inventory, purchases, and store performance without opening the Odoo dashboard.
