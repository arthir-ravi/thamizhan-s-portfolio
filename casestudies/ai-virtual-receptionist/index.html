AI VIRTUAL RECEPTIONIST

Video, Voice & Chat Reception Automation

I developed an AI Virtual Receptionist, an intelligent automation solution built with TrueGen AI and n8n for hospitals, clinics, educational institutions, salons, corporate offices, and service centers. The platform handles inbound video calls, voice calls, and chats, enabling appointment booking, availability checks, and information requests through natural conversations.

With Google Calendar integration, the system provides real-time scheduling, appointment confirmations, and seamless interaction management. It reduces receptionist workload, improves customer experience, and offers a scalable solution that can be adapted across multiple industries.

AI VIRTUAL RECEPTIONIST FEATURES

Natural Conversational Interaction

Engages users through human-like voice, video, and chat conversations, understanding requests naturally while delivering accurate and contextual responses.
